Biography

Egyptian electrical power engineer born in 1990. B.Sc., M.Sc., and Ph.D. all from Cairo University (2012, 2017, 2023). Research focuses on advanced control frameworks for low-inertia isolated microgrids with renewable energy integration — LFC, AVR, LADRC, fractional-order control, and AI-driven energy management. Currently on postdoctoral mission at HITSZ, China, developing the UCCMF framework.
